UKZN School of Music

A Legacy in Sound

This profile video captures the rich legacy and dynamic evolution of the UKZN School of Music, tracing its journey from its founding in the 1970s to its current role as a hub for diverse musical exploration and education.

We set out to create a visually engaging and emotionally resonant piece that reflects the institution’s commitment to cultural heritage, academic excellence, and musical innovation.

Narrative Approach

The video opens with archival references to the School’s early beginnings—when it was among the first in South Africa to formally offer music studies with an African focus. Through interviews with long-serving faculty and visuals of vintage photographs, we establish a sense of history and purpose.

As the story progresses, we shift into the present-day vibrancy of the school—capturing rehearsals, live performances, and classroom sessions across genres including classical, jazz, African traditional, contemporary, and electronic music.

Key Elements:

  • Historical context paired with present-day visuals
  • Interviews with lecturers, students, and alumni
  • Scenes of ensemble performances, studio work, and music technology labs
  • Emphasis on diversity, creativity, and community

Outcome

The video serves as a promotional and archival tool for the School of Music, showcasing its depth, heritage, and relevance to both local and international audiences. It positions UKZN as a forward-thinking institution rooted in tradition, offering a rich space for musicians to grow across a wide spectrum of genres.
